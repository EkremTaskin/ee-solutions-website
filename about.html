About us

Built by two people who were tired of watching small businesses lose to bad software.

E&E Solutions exists because the tools available to small businesses are either too generic, too complex, or too expensive. We fix that.

Our story

Why we started E&E.

We spent years watching small businesses struggle with the same problems. Generic software with features they didn't need. Expensive consultants who disappeared after delivery. Websites that looked like everyone else's.

The businesses that needed help the most — 10, 30, 100 person companies — were stuck. Too small for enterprise solutions. Too complex for off-the-shelf tools. Not sure where to start.

We started E&E to be the answer for those businesses. Tailored solutions scoped to fit. Clear deliverables, founder-led from first call to launch.

We serve clients across the US, work 100% remotely, and believe the best client relationships are built on clarity, not hand-waving.

Our values

How we work.

01

Honest before comfortable

If your idea won't work, we'll tell you. If we're not the right fit, we'll say so. We'd rather lose a project than set you up for a bad outcome.

02

Fixed scope, always

We scope everything in writing before we start. No surprises on the invoice. No scope creep baked in. What the proposal says is what you pay.

03

Simple over clever

We build the simplest solution that solves your problem. Unnecessary complexity is expensive to maintain and expensive to fix. We skip it.

04

You own what we build

Everything we build belongs to you after final payment. No licensing fees. No lock-in. You can take it to any other developer or run it yourself.
