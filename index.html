Common questions

Everything you want to know.

How fast can you start?
For most projects, we can start within 1–2 weeks of signing. The Operations Audit can begin as fast as 3 days after kickoff. We'll confirm your exact start date in the proposal.
What if my needs don't fit a pack?
The packs are starting points, not rigid boxes. We scope every project custom — if your needs overlap multiple packs or fall outside them, we'll put together a proposal that fits exactly what you need. Book a call and we'll figure it out together.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
Every project includes 30 days of free support after launch. After that, we offer optional ongoing retainers covering maintenance, updates, and priority support. Retainer pricing is scoped per project.
What tools do you integrate with?
We work with most common business tools: QuickBooks, HubSpot, Salesforce, Airtable, Notion, Slack, Google Workspace, Zapier, and many more. We'll ask about your stack in the strategy call and confirm feasibility before scoping.
Do you work with clients remotely?
Yes — 100% remote, and we serve clients across the US. We collaborate via video calls, Slack, and Loom updates. No travel needed, no location limitations.
