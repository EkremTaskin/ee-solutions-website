Pack FAQ

Do I need to prepare anything before the audit?
No prep required. We'll send a short pre-call questionnaire to gather basics. During discovery sessions, we ask the questions — you just talk through how work gets done today.
Can the audit lead directly into an implementation project?
Yes — and many clients do exactly that. The recommendations doc becomes the spec for a follow-on project. If you move into a follow-on pack, the audit fee applies as a credit toward the project.
Can the audit credit roll into a follow-on project?
Yes. The first 5 clients get a special founding rate in exchange for a testimonial after delivery. Spots are limited. Book a call to claim yours.
